About

Meet Granny
Mabel

Granny Mabel was the kind of woman whose presence warmed a room like fresh bread straight from the oven. Her silver hair, always tied in a loose bun, framed a face full of gentle wrinkles—each one a souvenir from a lifetime of laughter, worry, and wisdom.

Her eyes, sharp as ever behind round spectacles, missed nothing, especially mischief. She wore floral aprons like armor and wielded a wooden spoon with the authority of a queen. Children adored her stories, always told by the hearth with a cup of cocoa in hand.

But behind her soft voice and generous smile lay a mind sharper than most—Granny Mabel had secrets, and she never forgot anything.
